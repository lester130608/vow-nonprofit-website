About Us

At The Voices of Wellness, we believe mental health is a right—not a privilege. Our vision is to build a more empathetic, informed, and emotionally resilient community.

We are committed to offering accessible, culturally sensitive, and compassionate support that honors each person's dignity. Our core values include compassion, equity, inclusion, and integrity.

Our team includes mental health professionals, community leaders, and volunteers who are passionate about social change and collective healing.

Our Programs

Individual & Group Therapy

Low-cost individual and group therapy sessions for those in need, providing professional mental health support in a safe and welcoming environment.

Psychoeducational Workshops

Educational workshops on stress management, grief, anxiety, self-esteem, and healthy relationships to empower our community with practical tools.

Community Support Circles

Healing events and support circles focused on emotional wellness, creating safe spaces for community connection and mutual support.

Professional Training

Training for community leaders, educators, and frontline workers on trauma-informed care, self-care, and mental health awareness.

Accessible Care for Everyone

Sliding Scale Fees

We are committed to making mental health care accessible to everyone, regardless of income or insurance status. We offer sliding scale fees based on household income and financial need.

How It Works:

  • All clients are invited to share their financial situation confidentially
  • Fees are determined on a case-by-case basis to reflect what is affordable for you
  • We offer low-cost sessions and free spots when funding allows
  • No one will be turned away due to lack of ability to pay
